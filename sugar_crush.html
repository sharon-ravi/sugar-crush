About Catering


Crafted with Love

This Confectionary was founded in 2021 by Christabel Sharon. It was time for us to open the cupboards and share our magic with the world. Our neighborhood bakery found its way into new neighborhoods near and far, we wrote cookbooks and helped people all over celebrate their most important moments.And while we have grown, a few things will always remain: open early and close late, a dollop of buttercream makes everything better, and our mission: make today sweeter than yesterday with a Lip Smacking experience.

Our Blueberry Pancakes became iconic and people lined up around the block for our Belgian Wafflw. There was even talk of Bangalore guys becoming fans of Scrambled eggs. Sugar Crush was a destination for locals and tourists alike.
